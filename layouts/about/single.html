What is Stacksr?

Stacksr is a technical knowledge base focused on embedded systems, AIoT, and open-source engineering. We believe in learning through depth — not surface-level overviews, but real implementation details that matter when you're writing firmware at 2 AM.

Who writes here?

Engineers who work close to the metal. The posts are based on hands-on work with microcontrollers, sensors, communication protocols, and the open-source tools that tie them together.
